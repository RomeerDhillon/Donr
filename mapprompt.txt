### 1. Role-Specific Views

### 2. Data Sources (Firestore)
#### Food Centers
- Collection: `centers`
- Fields: `name`, `address`, `lat`, `lng`, `hours`, `centerType`, `phone`, `capacity`, `createdAt`

#### Donations
- Collection: `donations`
- Fields: `donorId`, `donorName`, `foodType`, `quantity`, `address`, `lat`, `lng`, `status`, `createdAt`

#### Food Requests
- Collection: `requests`
- Fields: `userId`, `foodType`, `urgency`, `lat`, `lng`, `createdAt`

---

- Tapping a marker opens a **bottom sheet** with details and actions:

  - Food Bank ‚Üí Accept / Reject Donations
